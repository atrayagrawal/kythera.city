How We Jam

1. Contribute

Everyone who's present participates. This adds their flavor to the jam and makes it better for the whole.

2. Make Friends

We sit in a circle by design. We look at each other, smile at each other, and end up making new friends through Kythera Jams.

3. Everyone is an Amateur

Everyone in the circle is an equal. There is no artist, no audience. And whatever someone is doing, they're doing it right.

These are core to us. And therefore, not everyone likes our kind of jams. And that's okay. :)
